A structured, transparent process designed to get you set up professionally, safely, and strategically from day one.

LEGAL & COMPLIANCE

Legal Documents You Will Sign

These agreements protect both you and Private Talent Group. We believe in full transparency.

Talent Management Agreement

The main contract outlining our working relationship, services provided, commission structure, and termination terms.

Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA)

Protects confidential information shared between you and the agency, including business practices and personal details.

Content License & Ownership Agreement

Important: You retain full ownership of all content you create. We receive a limited license to promote and monetize it on your behalf during our partnership.

2257 Compliance Agreement

Required for adult content compliance under U.S. law. Includes age verification and record-keeping acknowledgment.

Privacy & Data Protection Agreement

Details how your personal information and content will be stored, used, and protected.

Commission & Payment Agreement

Outlines revenue split, payment schedule, chargeback handling, and how net revenue is calculated.

W-9 Tax Form & Banking Authorization

Required for tax purposes and to set up secure payment processing to your account.

• Analytics and conversion tracking setup

• First-week performance review
